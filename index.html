About Me

I have my Master’s in Biomedical Engineering from Drexel University, after completing my Bachelor’s in Mechanical Engineering there in 2023. I’ve had the opportunity to work on some exciting projects like developing 3D-printed spinal implants with the Drexel Implant Research Center to implementing collaborative robotics in manufacturing for our senior design industry partner. I’ve also interned at Horiba Instruments, where I helped optimize optical testing systems for OEM spectrometers. I’m always looking for ways to bring creativity, precision, and purpose to every project I’m a part of, and I’m passionate about creating innovative solutions that make a real impact.

Outside of work, I am a hobby photographer, I like to go on runs, tutor math and engineering, play guitar, volleyball, soccer, game with friends, photography, and explore the city and one day the world!
