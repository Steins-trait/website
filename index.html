About Me

I have my Master’s in Biomedical Engineering from Drexel University, after completing my Bachelor’s in Mechanical Engineering there in 2023. I’ve had the opportunity to work on some exciting projects — from developing 3D-printed spinal implants with the Drexel Implant Research Center to implementing collaborative robotics in manufacturing at B&G Manufacturing. I’ve also interned at Horiba Instruments, where I helped optimize optical testing systems for OEM spectrometers. I’m always looking for ways to bring creativity, precision, and purpose to every project I’m a part of, and I’m passionate about creating innovative solutions that make a real impact.

Outside of work, I like to tutor math and engineering, play guitar, volleyball, soccer, game with friends, photography, and explore the city and one day the world!
